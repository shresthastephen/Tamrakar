Who we are

GigaGenius

A space where young children are introduced to the basics of academic knowledge, social interaction, and emotional development in a playful and nurturing environment. Through hands-on activities, imaginative play, and guided learning, GigaGenius develop essential skills such as language, math, creativity, and cooperation. We aims to inspire confidence in parents and excitement in children, showcasing the school as a place where dreams are taken seriously and big milestones are achieved.
